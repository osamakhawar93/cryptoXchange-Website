How much time must I wait until the coins appear in my address?

Following payment, the time it takes to get your coins depends on the payment option you selected. If you paid via SOFORT, NETELLER, EPS or OBT, Cryptoxchange gets an immediate confirmation after you complete the payment.

This means that your Coin/s will appear your wallet within a few seconds of execution. Once you send your SEPA payment, it often takes one business day to show up in our bank account. Once your SEPA transaction arrives, your coin/s are then instantly sent directly to your wallet. Cryptoxchange gives you the ability to check and monitor the status of your order at all times.

What Information and Documents are required for the Verification process?

You may verify your account by submitting a government issued ID (driver's license, passport). Both sides of the identification document will be required for verification. We will also require proof of residency by requesting the user submit a high-quality scanned image of a paper document issued within the last three months that is addressed to the user’s name and home address.

A bank statement or a utility bill for utilities consumed at the user’s home or a tax return will also do. We will also accept the certificate of residency issued by a government or a local government authority. Credit card statements, electronic bills or statements captured with online screenshots or mobile phone bills will not be accepted. Furthermore, all images have to be 300 DPI or higher resolution and documents must be visible in their entirety. Once the verification process is complete, the user will receive an email notification with either an approval or denial of access to the exchange.

What’s a mining fee? Must I add it?

All transactions within the Bitcoin network is, in theory, is done on a voluntary basis.

However, you are required to include a miners-fee in your transaction since this is the only way of processing your transaction in real-time. Modern wallets calculate the miner-fee automatically and implement them accordingly

How can I get a Bitcoin address if I don’t have one?

There are two options; 1. You can download a Bitcoin wallet on your mobile device 2. Or, you can have a wallet for online payments exclusively on your browser. Additionally, you will still have a bitcoin wallet in your Cryptoxchange account. If you would like to arrange an external wallet, then selecting your wallet can be executed within one minute. See https://bitcoin.org/en/choose-your-wallet to which wallet is right for you. Make sure you are well versed with regards to the risks and how to manage digital assets inside a wallet.
